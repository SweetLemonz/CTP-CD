Project Built on Unity Version 2019.2.0f1

Press E on NPC/Items to Interact when stood next to them to interact.

Red NPC Cube has a Condition for 2 Apple Items(which are spheres found 
next to the closest house of the Red NPC and inbetween the camping tents.

Blue NPC has a Condition for if you have spoken to the Red NPC first or not.
